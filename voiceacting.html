My Voice acting

As a budding indie games designer its important to have a plethora of skills that can be useful to your studio. From a young age I have been quite talented with my voice, whether that be doing character voices and creature noises or narration, I’ve even been known to sing from time to time (thought I won’t be posting that here).

Disembodied

The disembodied audio drama project was the first voice acting work that I took part in. Playing several voices including the zombie scream, the panicked male and the caution warning at the beginning, it was a fun experience to take on and to see the project come to fruition. the following audio is the project in its finished state, with thanks to Natasha Monica Barba-Castagnaro the producer, director and editor of the project.
